Group Outings

Source Pixabay

we get it

Outings are always more fun with a friend. Or two. Or a dozen. Below we've listed a few groups from Cornell that are heading to the festival together.If you don't want to go alone, or maybe just want to meet some new friends, maybe give them a chance.

Groups and Clubs

Nordic Ski

3:00 - 6:00 on Saturday

In between running sprints and tearing up snow, the Nordic Ski club has decided to explore Applefest, and they want you to join them. The club will meet at the Bernie Street Pavilion, newcomers are welcome.

Becker House

2:30 on Saturday

Join the best house on West Campus to enjoy Ithaca's annual celebration of all things fall. Meet by the House Office at 2:30pm on Saturday to join us.

LGBTQ Resource Center

10am on Sunday

Join us for a day of laughter, acceptance, and (of course) apple cider donuts. Meet us outside 626 Thurston to take a bus to the Commons, all identies welcome.

rose house gorge tour

9:45 am on Saturday

Join the Botanical Gardens staff on a nature-filled hike to the commons. Along the way you'll learn about the Cascadilla gorge system, enjoy a bit of nature, and reward yourself with some cider. Tours will leave from Rose House.

best buddies cornell

11:30 - 1:30 on Sunday

Take a trip with us to the Ithaca Apple Festival and have a great time while serving your new community. We will meet at the Bernie Street Pavilion in the Commons. Hope to see you there!
