Source: Unsplash

fun for the whole family

carnival

Fun houses, cotton candy, carnival games, oh my. Located at the end of the commons, our miniature carnival cannot fail to please.

Craft Show

Over 100 artists, craftsmen, artisans, makers, and just all around cool people have come together to share their work. Come browse extraordinary pieces of metalwork, woodwork, knit goods, fudge-making, and so much more.

farmer showcase

Come and learn about farming from the experts. From questions on how to live more sustainably to simply keeping a better garden, our collection of farm workers and teachers are happy to help.
