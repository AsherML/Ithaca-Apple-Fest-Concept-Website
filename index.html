about the festival

community celebration

Apple Fest is a time for all of Ithaca to come together to celebrate the changing of the seasons. Whether you're just starting your first year at college, looking forward to your last year in Ithaca, or have lived here all your life, Apple Fest is a sign that we're all one Ithaca.

Family Fun

Apple Fest offers many activities geared towards the whole family. Besides the standard booths (although kids are certainly welcome and expected at the apple cider donut stall), young children can enjoy face painting, arts and crafts, carnival games, and a safe environment while at the festival.

Great Eats

Apple Fest is filled with some of the best food Ithaca has to offer. Restaurants and vendors from across the town have come out to offer you their best dishes, provide a few free samples, and help you celebrate the changing of the seasons with a full belly.
